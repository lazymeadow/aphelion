Player Avatars: Player clothing will be customizable. Dyes are a possibility, but preferable is getting your clothes from a player who is a tailor. Players can visit a barber, makeup artist, or plastic surgeon to make changes to their avatar after creation. The more skilled a player is in these professions, the more options they have for customization.

Items will also be customizable, whether by altering the stats of the item or the appearance (for applicable items). A player can have a vehicle, or a spaceship, and these can also be customized.
