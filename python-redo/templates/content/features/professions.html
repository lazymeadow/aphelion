Players will have the ability to adopt (a) profession(s), which can include those that create goods. The process of crafting goods will be interactive, like a minigame (but relevant). It will be more interesting than the usual grind.

Examples: Armor/Weaponsmith/Gunsmith, Farmer, Tailor, Architect, Cook, Artist, Engineer

Professions will also include those that provide services.

Examples: Doctor, Barber, Makeup Artist, Plastic Surgeon, Mechanic, Musician, Dancer

Players can maintain storefronts/offices to provide their goods/services. There will also be public markets and auctions.

There is also a possiblity of military/government careers. Of course, that means you can be a criminal, smuggler, or whatever. You can steal (with consequences).

    Possible opportunities:
  • Deliveries
  • Resource brokerage
  • Player stores and marketplaces
  • Missions
  • Crime/Smuggling
  • Police/Military work
  • NPC Quests
  • Entertainment
  • Bartending
  • Doctor/Medicine
  • Crafting
  • Services
  • Real Estate
